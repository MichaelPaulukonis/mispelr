ReSpeller

Hello, world!

ReSpeller mangles your text in a variety ways.

Options

  • Maledict from a dictionary of common misspellings
  • Homophilia sounds like a homophone
  • Awfowell vowels are exteeeeendeed (at random)
  • ContonantSorry consonants are duplicated (at random)
  • Maxmister words (longer than 2 letters) are mixed about
